Introduction - Welcome to the class!

Syllabus

  • Participation grade
    • Attendance is required and graded
    • Weekly agile tracking reports
    • Pull requests
  • Everyone needs a client (web dev) or testers (game dev)

Review Design

  • Resubmit design documents
    • Make sure the design documents touch on each point

Use Agile methodology

  • User Stories, Sprints, Backlog, Burn-down charts
  • Each person will be Scrum Master for 2 weeks
  • Use a web site for Agile project management
  • Visual Studio Online: https://www.visualstudio.com
  • Pivotal Tracker: http://www.pivotaltracker.com

Teamwork - collaboration plan

  • Who was responsible for each part of the design document?
  • What each person's role will be?
    • Feature based roles preferred over skill based roles
    • How will you ensure workload is evenly distributed?
  • How will you make decisions?
  • When and where will you meet outside of class?
  • Make a schedule of scrum masters
  • What development, collaboration, and project management tools will you use?
  • Who is your client, or who are your testers (TBD is OK)
  • How often you will meet with your client or testers?
